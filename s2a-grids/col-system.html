S2a Column Grid

COL container logic ranges: 12 cols (1440+ - 768), 6 cols (up to 768px)

Container 12 cols (1440+ - 768), 6 cols (up to 768px)
• Standard: Max 1440px content container.
• HD: Max 1920px content container.
• Hero offset: Max 1920px content container, but margin tokens at breakpoints.
• Fluid: No max content container. (wip) maybe carousel or sliders etc. 2560 max
• No container: Full width/w overflow (wip) flex grid + object-fit etc.

Margins
• Hero offset: margin tokens used at breakpoints are: --spacing-4xl, --spacing-2xl, --spacing-lg.
• Margin rules are based on % inheritance (auto). Desktop/tablet (1440+-768) is Using 83.4% rules for now.
• Margins on mobile (767-360) we will use a spacing token.--grid-margin = --spacing-lg

Breakpoints
• XL 12 cols (1440+ HD) - stress test ie 2560 HD (used only as needed)
• L 12 cols (1440 - 1280) - most standard desktop range
• M 12 cols (1279 - 768) - Tablet landscape (font shift, ability to customize as needed, mostly “tablet-desktop” feel)
• S 6 cols (up to 767px) - true mobile, but covers tablet portrait as well. Think mobile simplified layouts or mobile experiences

5. Breakpoint Layouts

Demonstrating how elements can adapt their proportions differently across desktop, tablet, and mobile breakpoints using `col-lg-*`, `col-md-*`, and `col-sm-*` classes.

Example A: 3-up to 2-up to Stacked

On Desktop (12 cols), we show 3 items in a row (4 cols each). On Tablet (12 cols), the first two take half the width (6 cols) and the third drops to a full row (12 cols). On Mobile (6 cols), they all stack (6 cols each).
